What is Lively Wallpaper





Microsoft doesn’t officially let you set a video wallpaper for your desktop. However, thanks to the availability of multiple third-party apps, you can accomplish that task without scratching your head. To set video wallpaper on Windows 11, we will be making use of a third-party app called Lively Wallpaper.

Lively Wallpaper is an open-source application available on the Microsoft Store that performs one simple task, i.e., allows the user to set videos, webpages, or GIFs as desktop wallpapers and screensavers. The good thing about this app is that it offers a decent selection of many live wallpapers to choose from and is set as video wallpaper on Windows 11. If you are not into the offered selection of wallpapers, then you can always add custom ones to the library.



AI is everywhere these days, and this app also offers an AI feature that helps you convert a static image into a dynamic wallpaper. This AI feature adds depth effects as you move the cursor over your desktop. Setting up video wallpapers using Lively Wallpaper doesn’t hog your system resources, and the support of Chromium Embedded Framework (CEF) lets you set any web page as your wallpaper.




How to set video wallpaper on Windows 11?



Let us go through the steps to understand how to use Lively Wallpaper to set video wallpaper on Windows 11. Note that the Lively Wallpaper app is around 830MB, so it may take some time to download and install on your PC.



1. Download the Lively Wallpaper app from the Microsoft store by pressing Get button.



2. Launch the app after it is installed on your PC.

3. Click on Plus icon on the top.





4. You get multiple options to choose from in the Add Wallpaper Section:





* You can choose a local file and set it as a video wallpaper.


* You can enter the URL of a YouTube video and set it as a video wallpaper.


* Also, you can create a dynamic wallpaper from scratch and add depth effects.



5. In our case, we will be using a local file. So, click on the Open Button.





6. Press OK in the next window that pops up.


7. Lively Wallpaper will take some time to process the video as a wallpaper.


8. Once the video is loaded, you can click on the 3-dot menu icon next to the wallpaper and choose Se as Wallpaper.





A good feature for many that the Lively Wallpaper app offers is that it also plays the sound you have to the video. Honestly, it depends from person to person and whether they want to hear the sound of the video background or not. However, the sound is muted as soon as you open another program.
